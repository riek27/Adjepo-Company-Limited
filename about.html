About Adjepo Company Limited

Our Story

Adjepo Company Limited was established with a strong commitment to delivering quality, professional, and technically sound services across South Sudan. Based in Yei Town, we started as a construction-focused company and have expanded into multiple sectors including borehole drilling, solar installation, truck hire, architectural services, computer training, and general supplies.

Through dedication, discipline, and adherence to engineering standards, we have successfully delivered projects for individuals, NGOs, institutions, and communities.

Our growth is driven by integrity, professionalism, and a deep understanding that quality work builds long-term reputation.
