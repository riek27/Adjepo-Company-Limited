About Adjepo Company Limited

Our Story

Adjepo Company Limited was founded with a clear vision: to provide professional, reliable, and high-quality services that meet the growing needs of South Sudan's communities. Headquartered in Yei Town, we have steadily grown into a multidisciplinary company offering construction, borehole drilling, solar installation, interior and exterior design, truck and car hire, computer training, secretarial services, architectural works, and general supplies.

Our journey is rooted in a deep commitment to local development. From civil works at Yei Civil Hospital (funded by MSF) to large-scale warehouse construction (60m x 40m), we have built a reputation for technical accuracy, integrity, and client satisfaction. In 2025, we expanded our services to include professional borehole drilling, bringing clean water access to more communities.

Today, Adjepo Company Limited stands as a symbol of quality and dedication—proudly South Sudanese, and driven by a passion to build a better future.

Meet Our Founder & CEO

The visionary behind Adjepo Company Limited

Santo Biakika John

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Santo Biakika’s journey began in Yei, South Sudan, where he witnessed firsthand the challenges his community faced in accessing quality infrastructure, clean water, and reliable services. After earning a degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Khartoum, he worked on major reconstruction projects across the region, gaining over 15 years of hands-on experience in construction, project management, and community development.

In 2015, driven by a deep desire to rebuild and uplift his homeland, Santo Biakika founded Adjepo Company Limited. Starting with small-scale construction and general supplies, he steadily grew the company into a multidisciplinary firm. Under his leadership, the company has completed critical infrastructure—including the MSF‑funded Yei Civil Hospital renovation and a massive 60m x 40m warehouse—while expanding into borehole drilling, solar energy, and vocational training.

Santo Biakika’s philosophy is simple: “Quality is never an accident; it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, and skilled execution.” He personally oversees every major project, ensuring technical accuracy, ethical practices, and lasting value for clients and communities. His vision continues to guide Adjepo Company Limited toward becoming South Sudan’s most trusted service provider.
