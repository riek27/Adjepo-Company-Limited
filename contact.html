Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote for my project?

Fill out our contact form with project details, or call us directly. We'll schedule a site visit (if in Yei) or a virtual consultation to understand your requirements before providing a detailed quote.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Yei Town but serve clients across South Sudan. For projects outside Yei, we arrange logistics and can mobilize teams to any location.

Do you offer borehole drilling services?

Yes, we introduced professional borehole drilling in 2025. We provide community, private, and institutional borehole drilling, as well as maintenance and repair.

What solar power systems do you install?

We install solar systems from 5KW to 50KW for homes and businesses. Our packages include panels, inverters, batteries, and full installation with after-sales support.

Do you provide computer training?

Yes, we offer ongoing computer training programs ranging from basic literacy to advanced professional courses. Certificates are provided upon completion.

What is your payment structure?

We typically work with milestone-based payments tailored to each project. Specific terms are discussed and detailed in our contracts to ensure transparency.
