About Adjepo Company Ltd

Adjepo Company Limited is a proudly South Sudanese-owned enterprise based in Yei Town, offering professional services in construction, borehole drilling, solar installation, interior/exterior design, truck hire, computer training, secretarial services, architectural works, and general supplies.

We are committed to delivering quality projects that meet technical standards while maintaining professionalism, integrity, and client satisfaction. Our team brings local expertise and a passion for community development to every project we undertake.
