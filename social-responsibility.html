Social Responsibility

Our Commitment to Community

At Adjepo Company Limited, we recognize the importance of giving back to the communities in which we operate. Our Social Responsibility initiatives are designed to create sustainable positive impact, improve lives, and support the growth and wellbeing of vulnerable groups.

We believe that true success is measured not only by the quality of our services but also by the positive change we bring to society. Through targeted programs and partnerships, we invest in the future of South Sudan—one child, one family, one community at a time.

Focus Area: Education for Orphans

Supporting children at Kijiji National Orphanage Center, Yei Town

One of the core areas of our social responsibility is supporting children in need. We have identified Kijiji National Orphanage Center in Yei Town as a key partner in our initiatives. Through this partnership, we provide financial support to help pay school fees for orphans enrolled in various schools across Yei.

Our goal is to ensure that every child has access to quality education, empowering them to build a better future. Education is the foundation for breaking the cycle of poverty, and we are proud to play a part in giving these children hope and opportunity.

How We Make a Difference

Tangible actions that create lasting impact

Financial Support

Covering tuition fees for orphans at local schools, ensuring uninterrupted access to education.

Mentorship & Guidance

Regular visits to the orphanage to provide mentorship, guidance, and encouragement to the children.

Community Engagement

Partnering with local institutions and stakeholders to strengthen support systems for vulnerable children.

Sustainable Impact

Ensuring that support contributes to long-term education, growth, and skill development.

Our Commitment

At Adjepo Company Limited, Social Responsibility is not just a program; it is part of our core values. We believe that investing in education and community welfare is a cornerstone for building a stronger, more resilient society. By supporting Kijiji National Orphanage Center, we hope to give every child a chance to thrive and succeed, creating a lasting impact in Yei and beyond.

Join Us in Making a Difference

If you would like to contribute or partner with us in our Social Responsibility initiatives, please contact us or visit the Kijiji National Orphanage Center to see how your support can change lives.
